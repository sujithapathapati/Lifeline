About Lifeline

Lifeline is a dedicated platform connecting blood donors with those in urgent need. Our mission is to save lives by making blood donation easy, accessible, and efficient.

Our Vision

To create a community where no one struggles to find life-saving blood in times of emergency.

How We Help

  • Connecting patients with available donors in their area
  • Providing real-time emergency requests
  • Encouraging voluntary blood donation
